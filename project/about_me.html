About Me

Hi there! My name is Jonathan Oliphant. I am the 2nd youngest of 10 kids, and I am from Cassville Missouri. I have always loved homecooked meals, since my Mom made them for us kids pretty much everyday growing up. I never truly appreciated the art of cooking and baking until right before I moved out to come out to Rexburg. Now that I'm living away from my family, I have fallen in love with creating delicious food for myself!
